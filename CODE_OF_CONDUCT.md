# Code of Conduct

MiWhisper welcomes contributions, bug reports, and technical disagreement. The bar is simple: be constructive, be specific, and do not make the project harder to maintain.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and technically clear.
- Critique ideas directly without attacking people.
- Assume good intent unless there is evidence otherwise.
- Keep feedback actionable.
- Respect privacy. Do not post credentials, private logs, or machine-local personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- Doxxing or publishing private information
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally derailing technical discussion
- Posting exploit details publicly when a private security report is more appropriate

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and release conversations in this repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that harms the project or its contributors. Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocking participation.

## Reporting

If the problem is a security issue, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).

For conduct problems, contact the maintainers through GitHub in the least public channel available.
